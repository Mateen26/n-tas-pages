NOOR TASBEEH
Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 11, 2026 · Last updated: March 26, 2026

Welcome to Noor Tasbeeh ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Your privacy is important to us. Noor Tasbeeh is designed with a privacy-first approach — your spiritual practice data stays on your device unless you use optional features that need a network, as described below.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally

Core app data is stored on your device using local storage (including MMKV). We do not operate a user account system and we do not sync your Zikr or counting data to our own servers. Local data includes, for example:

  • Tasbeeh session counts, history, and statistics
  • Selected Zikr and custom Zikr entries
  • Daily goals and streak data
  • App preferences (theme, language, haptics, sound settings)
  • Bead skin selection and related unlock state
  • Reminder settings (including Zikr reminder times, Hadith notification preferences, and Azan-related options such as calculation method and manual city selection)

1.2 Optional Location (Azan / Prayer Alerts)

If you enable Azan (prayer time) notifications and choose automatic location, the app requests foreground location permission. With your consent, the device may obtain approximate coordinates to calculate prayer times. That location is used on the device and may be sent to a third-party prayer time API (see Section 4) only to retrieve prayer times—not to our servers. We do not build a profile of your movements and we do not store your location history on our systems.

If you choose a manual city instead, fixed coordinates from a preset list are used; the device location permission is not required for that path.

1.3 Data We Do NOT Collect on Our Servers

We do not require an account and we do not maintain a central database of your Zikr content or counts. We do not intentionally collect:

  • Name, email address, or phone number through the app
  • Contacts, call logs, photos, or your camera roll
  • Your general web browsing history inside a browser we control (the app is not a web browser)

Payment or refund handling—if you purchase an in-app product in the future—is processed by Apple or Google according to their policies; we do not receive your full payment card number through the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

Locally stored data is used to:

  • Display your session history and statistics inside the app
  • Track daily goals and streaks
  • Remember your preferences and custom Zikr entries
  • Schedule local notifications you turn on (Zikr reminders, optional Hadith reminders, optional Azan alerts)
  • Support optional features such as temporary bead unlocks after you choose to watch a rewarded video

Network requests we initiate are limited to features described in this policy (for example, prayer time lookup, optional update checks, ad delivery through Google, and optional product analytics through Microsoft Clarity in production builds—see below).

3. Data Storage & Security

Your app content and preferences are stored using MMKV and related on-device storage. In summary:

  • Core app data: Stored on your device; we do not operate a cloud backup of your Zikr or session history.
  • Device-level protection: Your device passcode, biometrics, and OS security apply to the device as a whole.
  • Product analytics: In production builds (not in developer preview builds), we use Microsoft Clarity to understand app usage and improve the product. Clarity may collect interaction and session-related data processed by Microsoft—see Section 4. Separately, Google Mobile Ads may use identifiers and diagnostics when serving ads—see Section 5.
  • Cookies: The mobile app does not use browser cookies; ad SDKs may use device advertising identifiers according to their policies.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may interact with the following categories of third parties (each has its own privacy policy):

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): Serves banner ads and rewarded video ads (for example, optional bead skin unlocks and streak restore). The SDK may process device and advertising identifiers. Where supported, we request non-personalized ads.
  • Microsoft Clarity: We use Microsoft Clarity in production builds to collect product analytics (such as how screens are used and session insights) to improve Noor Tasbeeh. Data is processed according to Microsoft’s Privacy Statement. Clarity is not initialized in local development builds.
  • Aladhan prayer times API (aladhan.com): If you use Azan notifications, prayer times may be fetched using coordinates or preset city data sent in API requests. We do not control that third-party service.
  • App update check: The app may fetch a small JSON file from our website (for example app-version.json) to compare your app version with a published latest version. This request does not include your Zikr data.
  • Apple & Google: App distribution, OS services, and optional in-app purchases are governed by their terms and privacy policies.
  • Expo / React Native tooling: Used to build the application.

We do not sell your personal information. Your locally stored Zikr text and session counts are not uploaded to us for advertising purposes.

5. Advertising

Noor Tasbeeh may show Google AdMob advertisements, including:

  • Banner ads on certain screens (for example, the main Tasbeeh screen and the Statistics screen)
  • Rewarded video ads when you explicitly choose to watch an ad for an optional in-app benefit (such as unlocking a bead style or restoring a streak)

AdMob’s technologies may collect or use device identifiers, ad interaction data, and related diagnostics to measure delivery and prevent fraud, as described in Google’s Privacy Policy and AdMob program policies. We configure requests for non-personalized ads where the platform allows. You can limit ad tracking using your device’s privacy settings (for example, “Limit Ad Tracking” on iOS or “Opt out of Ads Personalization” on Android).

If we offer a paid or subscription ad-free option in the future, details will appear in the app and in our Terms of Service.

6. Children's Privacy

Noor Tasbeeh is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly ask children for name, email, or contact details. Parents and guardians should be aware that, like most mobile apps, advertising SDKs and optional network features (as described above) may process device-level data. We encourage supervision for younger users when using online features.

7. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Reset Today's Stats: Clear today's session data from Settings
  • Clear All History: Permanently delete all session records from Settings
  • Uninstall: Removing the app will delete all stored data from your device

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data (visible within the app)
  • Delete your data at any time (via Settings)
  • Use the app without providing any personal information
  • Opt out of notifications at any time

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected within the app and on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
