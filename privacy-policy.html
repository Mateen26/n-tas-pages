NOOR TASBEEH
Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 11, 2026

Welcome to Noor Tasbeeh ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Your privacy is important to us. Noor Tasbeeh is designed with a privacy-first approach — your spiritual journey stays private.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally

All your personal data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Noor Tasbeeh session counts and history
  • Selected Zikr and custom Zikr entries
  • Daily goals and streak data
  • App preferences (theme, language, haptics, sound settings)
  • Selected bead skin customization
  • Reminder settings and times

1.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Browsing history
  • Financial or payment information

2. How We Use Your Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we use it solely to:

  • Display your Noor Tasbeeh session history and statistics
  • Track your daily goals and streaks
  • Save your app preferences and customizations
  • Provide a personalized spiritual experience
  • Send local reminders (if enabled by you)

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using MMKV, a high-performance key-value storage solution that operates entirely on your device. We employ the following measures:

  • Local-Only Storage: No cloud sync, no server uploads
  • Device-Level Protection: Your data is protected by your device's built-in security (PIN, fingerprint, face unlock)
  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools
  • No Cookies: The app does not use cookies or web tracking

4. Third-Party Services

Noor Tasbeeh may use the following third-party services:

  • Expo / React Native: Framework for building the app
  • Google Play Store / Apple App Store: For app distribution only

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

5. Advertising

The free version of Noor Tasbeeh may display advertisements. If ads are shown:

  • Ad networks may collect anonymized, non-personal device information
  • Premium users enjoy an ad-free experience
  • We do not share your Noor Tasbeeh data with advertisers

6. Children's Privacy

Noor Tasbeeh is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, the app is safe for use by minors under parental supervision.

7. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Reset Today's Stats: Clear today's session data from Settings
  • Clear All History: Permanently delete all session records from Settings
  • Uninstall: Removing the app will delete all stored data from your device

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data (visible within the app)
  • Delete your data at any time (via Settings)
  • Use the app without providing any personal information
  • Opt out of notifications at any time

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected within the app and on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Company: DevInspect
