FAQ

Is my data private?

Yes. All your session data, preferences, and custom Zikr are stored locally on your device. We never collect, transmit, or store your data on our servers.

Do I need an account?

No. Noor Tasbeeh works fully offline without any sign-up. All features are available as soon as you install the app.

How do I reset my stats?

Go to Settings → Data & History. You can reset today's stats or clear all history. Both actions are irreversible.

Can I add my own Zikr?

Yes. In the Zikr tab, tap the + button to add custom Zikr with Arabic text, transliteration, and translation.

Are reminders reliable?

Reminders use local notifications. For best results on Android, disable battery optimization for Noor Tasbeeh in your device settings.
